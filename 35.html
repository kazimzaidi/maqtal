The Fourth Gathering
The Condemnation by the People of the Faiths against the Killers of Imam al-Hussein (peace be upon him)
So for the pure ones from the household of Muhammad and Ali—may God's blessings be upon them both and upon their families—let the weepers weep, and for them let the mourners lament, and for the like of them let the tears flow, and let the criers cry out, and let the wailers wail, and let the lamenters lament. Where is the chosen one after the chosen one? Where are the rising suns? Where are the shining moons? Where are the radiant stars? Where are the banners of religion and the foundations of knowledge (2)?

عن بلده.
Al-Qanduzi al-Hanafi narrated from the Maqtal of Abu Mikhnaf that when Imam Zayn al-Abidin (peace be upon him) arrived in Medina from captivity, he addressed the people of Medina, saying in his sermon: O people, we have become displaced, driven away, expelled, far from our homelands, without any crime we had committed, nor any wrong we had perpetrated, nor any breach in Islam we had caused, nor any indecency we had done. By God, had the Prophet (peace be upon him and his family) commanded them to make war upon us, they could not have done more than what they did to us. Surely we belong to God, and to Him we shall return (1).

And how excellent is what al-Sayyid Muhammad Husayn al-Qazwini, may mercy be upon him, says:
    ولله درّ السيِّد محمد حسين القزويني عليه الرحمة إذ يقول :
Where is the protection, when in the plains your blood
was shed by the sword of Umayya and its spear?
Where is the protection, when these your dismembered bodies
remained three days in the scorching heat of its wastes?
Where is the protection, when these your sons
lie slain, their necks plundered by the swords?
Where is the protection, when these your children
were slaughtered thirsting in the soil of its courtyards?
Where is the protection, when these your maidens
were carried upon the camels' bare backs among its enemies—
carried, against the will of religion, while bereaved,
tearful, repeating their groaning sighs?

So who shall console Fatima, after Ahmad,
for the killing of her sons and the captivity of her daughters? (1)
فَمَنِ المعزّي بَعْدَ أحمدَ فاطماً في قَتْلِ أبناها وسَبْيِ بَنَاتِها (1)
Sheikh al-Saduq, may mercy be upon him, narrated from Abu al-Jarud, from Abu Abd Allah (peace be upon him), who said: The Prophet (peace be upon him and his family) was in the house of Umm Salama, and he said to her: Let no one enter upon me. Then al-Hussein (peace be upon him) came, and he was a child, and she could not restrain him until he entered upon the Prophet (peace be upon him and his family). Umm Salama entered after him, and there was al-Hussein upon his chest, and there was the Prophet weeping, and in his hand was something he was turning over.
    روى الشيخ الصدوق عليه الرحمة عن أبي الجارود  ، عن أبي عبدالله ( عليه السلام ) قال : كان النبي ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) في بيت أم سلمة  ، فقال لها : لا يدخل عليَّ أحد  ، فجاء الحسين ( عليه السلام ) وهو طفل فما ملكت معه شيئاً حتى دخل على النبيّ ( صلى الله عليه وآله )   ، فدخلت أمّ سلمة على أثره  ، فإذا الحسين على صدره  ، وإذا النبيُّ يبكي  ، وإذا في يده شيء يقلِّبه.
The Prophet (peace be upon him and his family) said: O Umm Salama, this is Gabriel, informing me that this one shall be slain, and this is the soil upon which he shall be killed. So keep it with you; when it turns to blood, then my beloved has been slain. Umm Salama said: O Messenger of God, ask God to ward that off from him. He said: I have done so, but God, mighty and majestic, revealed to me that for him there is a rank that none of the created beings shall attain, and that he has followers who will intercede and whose intercession will be accepted, and that the Mahdi shall be from his offspring. So blessed is he who is among the friends of al-Hussein and his followers—they, by God, are the triumphant on the Day of Resurrection (2).
    فقال النبيّ ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) : يا أمَّ سلمة  ، إن هذا جبرئيل يخبرني أن هذا مقتول  ، وهذه التربة التي يقتل عليها  ، فضعيها عندك  ، فإذا صارت دماً فقد قتل حبيبي  ، فقالت أم سلمة : يا رسول الله  ، سل الله أن يدفع ذلك عنه  ، قال : قد فعلت  ، فأوحى الله عزَّ وجلَّ إليَّ أنَّ له درجة لا ينالها أحد من المخلوقين  ، وأن له شيعة يشفعون فيشفَّعون  ، وأن المهديَّ من ولده فطوبى لمن كان من أولياء الحسين وشيعته  ، هم ـ والله ـ الفائزون يوم القيامة (2) .
And from Anas: the angel of rain sought permission to come to the Messenger of God, and the Prophet (peace be upon him and his family) said to Umm Salama: Hold the door for us; let no one enter upon us. Then al-Hussein (peace be upon him) came to enter, and she prevented him, but he leapt until he entered, and he began leaping upon the shoulders of the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) and sitting upon them. The angel said to him: Do you love him? He said: Yes. He said: Then your nation shall kill him, and if you wish I will show you the place where he shall be slain. So he stretched out his hand, and there was red clay. Umm Salama took it and tucked it into the corner of her veil. Thabit said: It reached us that it was the place where he was slain at Karbala (3).
    وعن أنس أن ملك المطر استأذن أن يأتي رسول الله  ، فقال النبيُّ ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) لأمِّ سلمة : املكي علينا الباب لا يدخل علينا أحد  ، فجاء الحسين ( عليه السلام ) ليدخل فمنعته  ، فوثب حتّى دخل  ، فجعل يثب على منكبي رسول الله ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) ويقعد عليهما  ، فقال له المَلَك : أتحبُّه ؟ قال : نعم  ، قال : فإن أمَّتك ستقتله  ، وإن شئت أريتك المكان الذي يُقتل فيه  ، فمدَّ يده فإذا طينة حمراء. فأخذتها أمُّ سلمة فصيَّرتها إلى طرف خمارها  ، قال ثابت : فبلغنا أنه المكان الذي قتل به بكربلاء (3) .
