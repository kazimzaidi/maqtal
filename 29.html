The narrator said, concerning what happened after the killing of Imam al-Hussein (peace be upon him): Then they turned to stripping al-Hussein (peace be upon him). Ishaq ibn Huwayya al-Hadrami took his shirt and wore it, and became leprous, and his hair fell out. It is narrated that there were found in his shirt some one hundred and ten marks, between arrow-shots, stabs, and blows (2). Imam al-Sadiq (peace be upon him) said: Found upon al-Hussein (peace be upon him) were thirty-three stabs and forty-four blows (3).

and an orphan girl, terrified for the body of her guardian,
