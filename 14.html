It is stated in the book Ibsar al-Ayn that Habib ibn Mazahir al-Asadi (peace be upon him) was a Companion who had seen the Prophet (God's blessings be upon him and his family), and he settled in Kufa and accompanied Ali (peace be upon him) in all his wars, and was among his close companions and the bearers of his knowledge. When Muslim ibn Aqil came to Kufa and stayed at the house of al-Mukhtar, and the Shia began to come and go to him, Habib and Muslim ibn Awsaja began to take the pledge of allegiance for Hussein (peace be upon him) in Kufa, until, when Ubaydullah ibn Ziyad entered Kufa, dissuaded its people from Muslim (peace be upon him), and his supporters fled, their clans concealed and hid the two of them. When Hussein (peace be upon him) arrived at Karbala, Habib and Muslim went out to him in concealment, traveling by night and hiding by day until they reached him.(2)
