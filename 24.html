The Fourth Gathering, of the Night of Ashura
The Calamity of the Prophet (peace be upon him and his family)
in al-Husayn (peace be upon him)
Sheikh al-Mufid, may God's mercy be upon him, narrated from Abdullah ibn Sinan, from Abu Abdullah Ja'far ibn Muhammad (peace be upon them), that he said concerning the slaughter of the Master of Martyrs (peace be upon him) and his household: Their slaughter weighs heavily upon the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family), and had he been alive in this world on that day, he (peace be upon him and his family) would have been the one consoled over them (1).

And may God have mercy on Mansur al-Numayri, when he says concerning the grief of the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) over his grandson and his sweet basil, al-Husayn (peace be upon him):
    ورحم الله منصور النمري إذ يقول في حزن رسول الله ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) على سبطه وريحانته الحسين ( عليه السلام ) :
Woe to you, O slayer of al-Husayn! You have
returned bearing a burden too heavy for the bearer;
What manner of gift have you given to Ahmad
in his grave — the burning of the bereaved mother?
Come then, and seek tomorrow his intercession,
rise and come to his pool along with the thirsty (2).

And al-Sharif al-Radi, may God's mercy be upon him, said:
    وقال الشريف الرضي عليه الرحمة :
Were the Messenger of God to live after him,
he would sit this day in mourning over him;
O Messenger of God, had you beheld them,
they between the slain and the captive,
your eyes would have seen a scene of them
a grief to the heart and a mote in the eye.

Sheikh al-Saduq, may God's mercy be upon him, narrated from Ibn Abbas, from the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family), in a tradition in which he said: As for al-Husayn, he is from me, and he is my son and my child, and the best of creation after his brother. He is the Imam of the Muslims, the master of the believers, the vicegerent of the Lord of the Worlds, the aid of those who seek aid, the refuge of those who seek shelter, and God's proof over all His creation. He is the master of the youth of Paradise, and the gate of salvation for the nation. His command is my command, and obedience to him is obedience to me. Whoever follows him is from me, and whoever disobeys him is not from me. When I saw him, I remembered what will be done to him after me. It is as though I see him having sought refuge in my sanctuary and my grave, yet finding no refuge. So I embrace him in his sleep to my chest, and I command him to depart from the land of my migration, and I give him glad tidings of martyrdom. So he departs from it to the land of his slaughter and the place of his fall — a land of grief and tribulation, of killing and annihilation. A band of Muslims will support him; those are among the foremost martyrs of my nation on the Day of Resurrection. It is as though I look upon him, struck by an arrow, falling slain from his horse. Then he is slaughtered as a ram is slaughtered, wronged. Then the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) wept, and those around him wept, and their voices rose in lamentation. Then he (peace be upon him and his family) rose, saying: O God, I complain to You of what my household will face after me. Then he entered his dwelling (1).

The First Gathering, of the Day of Ashura
The Household (peace be upon them) and the Day of Ashura
So for the pure ones from the household of Muhammad and Ali — may God's blessings be upon them and their families — let the weepers weep, and for them let the mourners lament, and for the like of them let tears be shed, and let the criers cry out, and the clamorers clamor, and the wailers wail. Where is al-Hasan, and where is al-Husayn? Where are the sons of al-Husayn — righteous one after righteous one, truthful one after truthful one? Where is the path after the path? Where is the chosen one after the chosen one? Where are the rising suns? Where are the shining moons? Where are the radiant stars? Where are the banners of the faith and the foundations of knowledge? (1).

أهل البيت ( عليهم السلام ) ويوم عاشوراء
    فعلى الأطائب من أهل بيت محمّد وعلي صلى الله عليهما وآلهما  ، فليبك الباكون  ، وإياهم فليندب النادبون  ، ولمثلهم فلتذرف الدموع  ، وليصرخ الصارخون  ، ويضجَّ الضاجون  ، ويعجَّ العاجون  ، أين الحسن وأين الحسين  ، أين أبناء الحسين  ، صالح بعد صالح  ، وصادق بعد صادق  ، أين السبيل بعد السبيل  ، أين الخيرة بعد الخيرة  ، أين الشموس الطالعة  ، أين الأقمار المنيرة  ، أين الأنجم الزاهرة  ، أين أعلام الدين وقواعد العلم (1) .
Al-Sayyid Ibn Tawus, may God's mercy be upon him, said concerning the Day of Ashura: When you resolve upon what is unavoidable of food and drink, after the time of the calamity has passed, say what means: O God, You have said: "And do not think of those who are killed in the way of God as dead.
