Upon the Family of God's Messenger may there be blessings—
angels of radiant faces, noble and pure.
O Fatima, my grief is for your exalted sons,
so I have grown gray, though I am, in truth, but a young man.
And I have come to find no pleasure in the sweetness of living,
as though all good things were forbidden to me.
They say to me: "Bear it with beautiful patience and consolation,"
but I have no will toward beautiful patience.
How can I be patient after the Family of Muhammad,
when in my heart for them is anguish and sickness? (1)
لآلِ رسولِ اللهِ صلَّت عليهِمُ ملائكةٌ بِيْضُ الوُجُوهِ كِرَامُ أَفَاطِمُ أشجاني بنوكِ ذوو العُلاَ فَشِبْتُ وَإِنّي صَادِقٌ لَغُلاَمُ وأصبحتُ لا ألتذُّ طِيْبَ مَعِيشَة كأنَّ عليَّ الطَّيِّبَاتِ حَرَامُ يقولونَ لي صبراً جميلا وسِلْوةً ومالي إلى الصَّبْرِ الجميلِ مَرَامُ فكيف اصطباري بَعْدَ آلِ محمَّد وفي القلبِ منهم لَوْعَةٌ وَسِقَامُ (1)
It is narrated from Ibn Ammara, from his father, from al-Sadiq, from his forefathers (peace be upon them), who said: The Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) said: God Almighty granted my brother Ali ibn Abi Talib (peace be upon him) virtues whose number none can count but Him. So whoever mentions one of his virtues, acknowledging it, God will forgive him his sins, both past and future, even were he to come on the Day of Resurrection bearing the sins of mankind and jinn. And whoever writes down one of the virtues of Ali ibn Abi Talib (peace be upon him), the angels will not cease asking forgiveness for him so long as any trace of that writing remains. And whoever listens to one of his virtues, God will forgive him the sins he committed through that hearing. And whoever looks upon a writing of his virtues, God will forgive him the sins he committed through that looking. Then the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) said: Looking upon Ali ibn Abi Talib (peace be upon him) is worship, and mentioning him is worship, and no servant's faith is accepted save through loyalty to him and disavowal of his enemies (2).
    روي عن ابن عمارة  ، عن أبيه  ، عن الصادق  ، عن آبائه ( عليهم السلام ) قال : قال رسول الله ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) : إن الله تعالى جعل لأخي علي بن أبي طالب ( عليه السلام ) فضائل لا يحصي عددها غيره  ، فمن ذكر فضيلة من فضائله مقرّاً بها غفر الله له ما تقدَّم من ذنبه وما تأخَّر ولو وافى القيامة بذنوب الثقلين  ، ومن كتب فضيلة من فضائل علي بن أبي طالب ( عليه السلام ) لم تزل الملائكة تستغفر له ما بقي لتلك الكتابة رسم  ، ومن استمع إلى فضيلة من فضائله غفر الله له الذنوب التي اكتسبها بالاستماع  ، ومن نظر إلى كتابة في فضائله غفر الله له الذنوب التي اكتسبها بالنظر  ، ثمَّ قال رسول الله ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) : النظر إلى علي بن أبي طالب ( عليه السلام ) عبادة  ، وذكره عبادة  ، ولا يُقبل إيمان عبد إلاَّ بولايته والبراءة من أعدائه (2) .

So the companions of the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) turned to him and said: Glad tidings to you for what the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) has announced! What did you do this day, that what was recorded was recorded for you? The man said: I am not aware that I did anything, except that I left my house intending to attend to a need that I had delayed, fearing it had slipped away from me. So I said to myself: I will make up for it by looking upon the face of Ali ibn Abi Talib (peace be upon him), for I have heard the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) say: Looking upon the face of Ali is worship. So the Messenger of God (peace be upon him and his family) said: Yes, by God, worship—and what worship! O servant of God, you went seeking to earn a dinar to feed your family, and that escaped you, so you made up for it by looking upon the face of Ali, while you love him and believe in his virtue. That is better for you than if the whole world were red gold for you and you spent it in the way of God. And you shall surely intercede, for the number of every breath you took on your way to him, on behalf of a thousand souls whom God shall free from the Fire through your intercession (1).

He said: Abu Abdullah (peace be upon him) replied: Far from it, far from it! Their backsides have missed the pit. Indeed, God has angels who roam about, apart from the noble recording angels. When they pass by a people who are mentioning Muhammad and the Family of Muhammad (peace be upon them), they say: Stop, for you have found what you sought. So they sit and study with them. From Yazid ibn Abd al-Malik, from Abu Abdullah (peace be upon him), who said: Visit one another, for in your visiting is the revival of your hearts and the remembrance of our traditions. And our traditions make you compassionate toward one another. If you take hold of them, you will be guided and saved; and if you abandon them, you will go astray and perish. So take hold of them, and I am the guarantor of your salvation.
    وعن يزيد بن عبد الملك  ، عن أبي عبدالله ( عليه السلام ) قال : تزاوروا فإن في زيارتكم إحياءً لقلوبكم  ، وذكراً لأحاديثنا  ، وأحاديثنا تعطِّف بعضكم على بعض  ، فإن أخذتم بها رشدتم ونجوتم  ، وإن تركتموها ضللتم وهلكتم  ، فخذوا بها وأنا بنجاتكم زعيم.
From al-Mustawrid al-Nakha'i, from one who narrated to him, from Abu Abdullah (peace be upon him), who said: Among the angels who are in the heavens, there are those who look down upon the one, the two, and the three who are mentioning the virtue of the Family of Muhammad (peace be upon him and his family). He said: They say: Do you not see these few, despite the multitude of their enemies, describing the virtue of the Family of Muhammad? He said: Then the other group of angels says: That is the bounty of God, which He grants to whom He wills; and God is the Possessor of great bounty.
