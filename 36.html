So I went out, with my guide leading me toward her house, and the people of Medina, men and women, came to her. When I reached her, I said: O Mother of the Believers! Why are you crying out and calling for help? She did not answer me, and she turned to the Hashimite women and said: O daughters of Abd al-Muttalib, mourn with me and weep with me, for by God your master and the master of the youths of Paradise has been slain. By God, the grandson of the Messenger of God and his sweet basil, al-Hussein, has been killed. I said: O Mother of the Believers, how do you know this? She said: I saw the Messenger of God just now in a dream, disheveled and terrified, and I asked him about his state, and he said: My son al-Hussein (peace be upon him) and the people of his household have been killed today, and I have buried them, and just now I finished burying them.

She said: So I arose and entered the house, scarcely able to reason, and I looked, and there was the soil of al-Hussein that Gabriel had brought from Karbala, and he had said: When this soil turns to blood, then your son has been slain.     قالت : فقمت حتى دخلت البيت وأنا لا أكاد أن أعقل  ، فنظرت فإذا بتربة الحسين التي أتى بها جبرئيل من كربلا  ، فقال : إذا صارت هذه التربة دماً فقد قُتل ابنك  ، وأعطانيها النبيُّ ( صلى الله عليه وآله ) فقال : اجعلي هذه التربة في زجاجة ـ أو قال : في قارورة ـ ولتكن عندك  ، فإذا صارت دماً عبيطاً فقد قتل الحسين  ، فرأيت القارورة الآن وقد صارت دماً عبيطاً تفور.
